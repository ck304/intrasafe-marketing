FAQ

Common questions.

Do volunteers need to download an app? +

No — IntraSafe Vigil is a PWA (Progressive Web App). Volunteers install it from their browser in seconds. No app store required.

Is it secure? +

Yes — all data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Role-based access control ensures volunteers only see what they need to see.

What happens if there's no internet? +

Core features work offline and automatically sync when the connection restores. Your team stays coordinated even in poor signal environments.

Can we customize it for our organization? +

Yes — Pro and Enterprise plans include custom zones, escalation tiers, playbooks, and branding. Enterprise adds multi-location support and denomination-level configuration.

How long does setup take? +

Most teams are fully configured and ready to run their first service in under 30 minutes. Our onboarding flow guides you step by step.
