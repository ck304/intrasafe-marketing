Our Story

A Message from Our Founders

Why we built IntraSafe, and the conviction that keeps us building it.

For decades we've served as business leaders, community organizers, and volunteer church leaders. And for just as long, we've carried a quiet concern — have we truly prepared ourselves and the people around us to respond when something goes wrong?

Not the emergencies we plan for. The ones we don't.

Built not from a boardroom, but from years of standing in lobbies, parking lots, and sanctuaries — after the drills, after the close calls, after the long conversations about what we'd do if something actually happened. And always, underneath it all, the quiet but heavy weight of responsibility for the people in our care.

IntraSafe is the tool we always wished we had.

We've built and scaled businesses from zero to hundreds of millions — and we've learned that growth without intention creates complexity that crushes the people it was meant to serve. So we made a different choice. We kept the team small, the costs low, and the focus sharp. Not because we had to. Because we wanted to build something with a real and lasting impact.

We designed IntraSafe to be robust, reliable, and affordable — because the organizations that need it most are often the ones with the least resources. From a small community gathering to a large multi-site organization, no one should have to choose between safety and budget.

We won't pretend we have everything figured out. But we have the experience to know what matters, and the conviction to keep making it better.

If you find value here, share it. The more widely this is used, the safer our communities become.

— The IntraSafe Team
